Spiritual Sound System

I've started seeking a certain type of listening lately, inspired by Knox Robinson. Music as a counterpoint to our current times. Positive vibrations and spiritual sensations.

The deeper I got the more I enjoyed it. The exploratory aspect. The listening experience.

So I made a thing to help share it: Spiritual Sound System.

The project kicked off with the 5 hour track/mix Music For Mushrooms: A Soundtrack For The Psychedelic Practitioner by East Forest.

This week is the new album from Horse Lords: The Common Task.

Enjoy some positive vibrations and stay healthy.
